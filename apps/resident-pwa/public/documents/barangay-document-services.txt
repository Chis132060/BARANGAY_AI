BARANGAY DOCUMENT SERVICES GUIDE
=================================

Document Title: Complete Guide to Barangay Document Services
Issued By: Barangay Office
Year: 2024

This guide covers all certificates and documents available at the Barangay Hall and
through the Smart Barangay PWA portal.

=== 1. CERTIFICATE OF INDIGENCY ===
PURPOSE: For residents with low income seeking assistance for medical, educational, or legal needs.
REQUIREMENTS:
  - Valid Government ID
  - Proof of low income (Sworn Affidavit or Certificate from DSWD/Social Worker)
  - Referral letter from requesting institution (hospital, school, court)
FEE: FREE (no processing fee)
PROCESSING TIME: 10-20 minutes walk-in; 1 business day online
VALIDITY: 3 months from date of issue
USE CASES: Hospital bills, burial assistance, scholarship application, legal aid

=== 2. CERTIFICATE OF RESIDENCY ===
PURPOSE: Confirms that the applicant is a registered resident of the barangay.
REQUIREMENTS:
  - Valid Government ID
  - Proof of Address: recent utility bill OR Lease Contract OR sworn affidavit by landlord
FEE: ₱30.00
PROCESSING TIME: 15-20 minutes walk-in; 1 business day online
VALIDITY: 6 months from date of issue
USE CASES: School enrollment, voter registration, opening bank accounts

=== 3. BARANGAY BUSINESS CLEARANCE ===
PURPOSE: Required before applying for a Mayor's Permit / Business License.
REQUIREMENTS:
  - DTI Certificate (sole proprietor) or SEC Registration (corporation/partnership)
  - Lease Contract or Land Title / Tax Declaration
  - Fire Safety Inspection Report (from BFP)
  - Environmental Compliance Certificate (for applicable businesses)
  - Sanitary Permit (for food businesses)
FEE:
  - Micro-enterprise (capital < ₱3M): ₱500
  - Small Business (capital ₱3M-₱15M): ₱1,000
  - Medium/Large Business: ₱2,000
PROCESSING TIME: 2-3 business days (requires inspection)
VALIDITY: 1 year (renewable annually)

=== 4. BARANGAY ID ===
PURPOSE: Official Barangay identification card for registered residents.
REQUIREMENTS:
  - Proof of Residency
  - 2 pcs 1x1 ID photo (white background)
  - Birth Certificate (for minors)
FEE: ₱50.00 (replacement ₱75.00)
PROCESSING TIME: 3-5 business days
VALIDITY: 3 years

=== 5. CERTIFICATE OF GOOD MORAL CHARACTER ===
PURPOSE: Certifies that the applicant has good standing in the community.
REQUIREMENTS:
  - Valid Government ID
  - 2 Character References from known community members
FEE: ₱50.00
PROCESSING TIME: 30 minutes to 1 business day
USE CASES: Job applications, scholarship, police clearance pre-requisite

=== 6. SOLO PARENT CERTIFICATE ===
PURPOSE: Entitles solo parents to benefits under RA 8972 (Solo Parents' Welfare Act).
REQUIREMENTS:
  - Birth Certificates of children
  - Proof of solo parent status (death certificate, annulment decree, etc.)
  - 2 pcs 2x2 ID photo
FEE: FREE
PROCESSING TIME: 3-5 business days (requires social worker assessment)
VALIDITY: 1 year (must be renewed annually)

=== 7. BARANGAY PROTECTION ORDER (BPO) ===
PURPOSE: Protects victims of domestic violence and abuse.
HOW TO FILE: Visit the Barangay Hall and speak with the VAWC Desk Officer or Barangay Captain.
REQUIREMENTS: Incident report + sworn statement of the victim
FEE: FREE
PROCESSING TIME: Same day (emergency); 24-48 hours otherwise
Note: BPO is effective for 15 days. For longer protection, refer to RTC for Temporary/Permanent Protection Order.

=== HOW TO APPLY ONLINE (Smart Barangay PWA) ===
1. Register/Log in to your resident account
2. Select the document you need from the AI Chat or Request section
3. Fill up the in-chat form sent by the AI
4. Upload required documents (clear photos or scanned copies)
5. Pay via GCash/Maya or at the Barangay Hall
6. Track your request status on the Updates page
7. Receive your document (pickup or Barangay courier delivery)

For assistance, ask the Smart Barangay AI or visit the Barangay Hall.
