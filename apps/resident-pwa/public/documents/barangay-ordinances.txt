BARANGAY ORDINANCES AND COMMUNITY RULES
=======================================

Document Title: Compilation of Barangay Ordinances
Issued By: Sangguniang Barangay
Year: 2024

=== ORDINANCE NO. 001-2024: CURFEW FOR MINORS ===
All minors (persons below 18 years of age) are prohibited from loitering, playing, or
wandering in public places, streets, or establishments without parent/guardian supervision:
  - Weekdays (Monday-Friday): 10:00 PM to 5:00 AM
  - Weekends & Holidays: 11:00 PM to 5:00 AM
Penalty: First offense - Warning and counseling. Second offense - ₱500 fine payable by parents.

=== ORDINANCE NO. 002-2024: NOISE AND NUISANCE REGULATION ===
- Use of sound systems, karaoke, and amplified music must cease by 10:00 PM on weeknights.
- On weekends and holidays, noise must stop by 11:00 PM.
- Construction activities are allowed only from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays.
- Penalty for violation: ₱1,000 first offense, ₱2,000 second offense, ₱5,000 + closure third offense.

=== ORDINANCE NO. 003-2024: SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT ===
- Garbage segregation is MANDATORY in all households and establishments.
  * Green bag / Green bin: Biodegradable (food scraps, garden waste)
  * Yellow bag / Yellow bin: Non-biodegradable (plastics, glass, metals)
  * Red bag / Red bin: Hazardous (batteries, chemicals, medicines)
- Garbage Collection Schedule:
  * Biodegradable: Every MONDAY and THURSDAY morning (6:00 AM - 9:00 AM)
  * Non-biodegradable: Every TUESDAY and FRIDAY morning (6:00 AM - 9:00 AM)
  * Hazardous: Every last Saturday of the month
- Littering Penalty: ₱1,000 to ₱5,000

=== ORDINANCE NO. 004-2024: COMMUNITY SMOKING BAN ===
- Smoking is strictly prohibited in all barangay-owned facilities, playgrounds, markets, and
  within 10 meters of any establishment entrance.
- Smoking areas are designated at specific spots indicated by signage.
- Penalty: ₱500 fine per violation (non-compoundable)

=== ORDINANCE NO. 005-2024: ANTI-ILLEGAL GAMBLING ===
- All forms of illegal gambling (jueteng, masiao, ending, etc.) are prohibited.
- Involvement carries criminal liability under PD 1602 and RA 9287.
- Rewards are given to informants who provide actionable tips leading to arrests.

=== ORDINANCE NO. 006-2024: ANTI-ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES IN PUBLIC ===
- Drinking alcoholic beverages in public streets, alleys, and plazas is PROHIBITED.
- Violation: ₱500 fine or 1-day community service.
- Establishments selling alcohol must observe designated hours and areas.

=== ORDINANCE NO. 007-2024: ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION ===
- Burning of garbage and waste materials is strictly prohibited.
- Unauthorized cutting of trees requires a barangay permit and DENR clearance.
- Water sources must be kept clean; dumping waste in waterways is penalized.

For questions or complaints, contact the Barangay Secretary at the Barangay Hall.
Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
