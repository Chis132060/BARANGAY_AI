BARANGAY CLEARANCE POLICY
=========================

Document Title: Barangay Clearance
Issued By: Office of the Barangay Captain
Effectivity: January 1, 2024

PURPOSE:
A Barangay Clearance is an official document issued by the Barangay certifying that the
requesting individual has no pending cases, complaints, or violations within the jurisdiction
of the Barangay. It is required for employment, government ID applications, bank accounts,
and business permits.

REQUIREMENTS:
1. Valid Government-Issued ID (any one of the following):
   - PhilSys ID (National ID)
   - SSS / GSIS ID
   - Passport
   - Driver's License
   - Voter's ID
   - Postal ID
2. Proof of Residency (any one of the following):
   - Recent utility bill (electricity, water, internet) - not older than 3 months
   - Lease/Rental Contract
   - Barangay Certificate of Residency
3. 1 piece 1x1 ID photo (recent, white background)
4. Accomplished Application Form (available at Barangay Hall or via this PWA portal)

PROCESSING FEE:
- Employed Applicants: ₱50.00
- Self-Employed / Business: ₱75.00
- Senior Citizens / PWD: FREE (must present valid Senior/PWD ID)
- OFW: ₱100.00 (includes notarization fee)

PROCESSING TIME:
- Walk-in: 15-30 minutes (subject to queue)
- Online Request (via PWA): 1-2 business days (for pickup or delivery)

VALIDITY:
- Barangay Clearance is valid for 6 months from date of issuance.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Applicants with pending cases or complaints will be referred to the Lupon Tagapamayapa.
- Clearance for minors must be requested by a parent or legal guardian.
- Lost clearances may be replaced for ₱30.00 with affidavit of loss.

OFFICE HOURS:
Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Saturday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Noon)
